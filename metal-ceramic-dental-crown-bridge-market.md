<p><p>The metal ceramic dental crown bridge market encompasses two main types: dental crowns and dental bridges. Dental crowns are prosthetic caps placed over damaged teeth, restoring functionality and aesthetics. They typically consist of a metal core covered with a ceramic layer for a natural appearance. Dental bridges, on the other hand, replace one or more missing teeth by anchoring artificial teeth to adjacent natural teeth or implants, using metal ceramic materials for durability and lifelike appearance. Together, they enhance oral health and smile aesthetics.</p></p>
