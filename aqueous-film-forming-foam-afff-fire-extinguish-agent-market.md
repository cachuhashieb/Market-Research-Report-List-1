<p><p>Aqueous Film Forming Foam (AFFF) is a specialized fire extinguishing agent primarily used to combat flammable liquid fires, particularly those caused by petroleum-based fuels. AFFF is widely employed in various sectors, including aviation, marine, industrial, and municipal firefighting operations.</p><p>The AFFF Fire Extinguish Agent Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 15% during the forecast period.
