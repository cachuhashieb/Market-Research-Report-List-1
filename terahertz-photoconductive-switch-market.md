<p><p>The Terahertz photoconductive switch market is divided into two primary types: Terahertz Emitter Switches and Terahertz Receiver Switches. Terahertz Emitter Switches are designed to generate terahertz radiation when stimulated by an electrical pulse, enabling applications in imaging and spectroscopy. Conversely, Terahertz Receiver Switches detect terahertz signals, converting them into usable electrical signals for analysis and processing. Both types are integral to advancing terahertz technologies across various fields, including telecommunications, security, and medical diagnostics.</p></p>
