<p><ul><li>Brushed</li><li>Brushless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cooling blower market is primarily divided into two types: brushed and brushless blowers. Brushed blowers utilize brushes to transfer power to the motor, offering a cost-effective solution with simpler design but potentially shorter lifespan due to wear on the brushes. In contrast, brushless blowers feature electronic commutation, which enhances efficiency, longevity, and performance by eliminating friction from brushes. This leads to quieter operation and reduced maintenance needs, making brushless blowers increasingly popular in various applications.</p></p>
