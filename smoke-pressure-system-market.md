<p><p>The smoke pressure system market comprises two primary types: roof-mounted and wall-mounted systems. Roof-mounted systems are installed on building rooftops and are designed to dissipate smoke and heat in the event of a fire, promoting safe evacuation. Conversely, wall-mounted systems are integrated into the building's walls and serve a similar purpose by directing smoke away from escape routes. Both types are essential for enhancing fire safety compliance, providing effective smoke control, and ensuring the well-being of occupants during emergencies.</p></p>
