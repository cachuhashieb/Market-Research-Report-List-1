<p><p>The color ultrasound market comprises two primary types: hand-carried color Doppler ultrasound systems and cart-based color Doppler ultrasound systems. Hand-carried systems are lightweight and portable, making them ideal for point-of-care settings like clinics and emergency rooms. In contrast, cart-based systems are larger and more sophisticated, providing advanced imaging capabilities and typically used in hospital environments for detailed examinations. Both types utilize color Doppler technology to visualize blood flow, aiding in cardiovascular assessments and diagnosis.</p></p>
