<p><ul><li>GEO satellite</li><li>LEO satellite</li><li>Sun-synchronous satellite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial satellite launch market is categorized primarily into three types: GEO (Geostationary Earth Orbit) satellites, LEO (Low Earth Orbit) satellites, and Sun-synchronous satellites. GEO satellites maintain a fixed position relative to the Earth, ideal for telecommunications and weather monitoring. LEO satellites orbit closer to the Earth, facilitating low-latency communications and earth observation. Sun-synchronous satellites orbit in synchronization with the sun, allowing consistent lighting conditions for imaging applications. These distinct markets cater to various technological and application needs in the satellite industry.</p></p>
