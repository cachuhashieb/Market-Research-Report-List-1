<p><p>The massive marine crane market encompasses various types, primarily including ship cranes and floating cranes. Ship cranes are integrated into vessels, providing efficient loading and unloading capabilities during maritime operations, often seen in cargo and container ships. In contrast, floating cranes are standalone units stationed on water, designed for heavy lifting tasks such as salvaging or construction projects near coastlines. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing operational efficiency and safety in marine logistics and infrastructure development.</p></p>
