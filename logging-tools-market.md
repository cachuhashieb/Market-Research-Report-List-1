<p><ul><li>Open Hole Logging</li><li>Cased Hole Logging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The logging tools market encompasses two primary applications: open hole logging and cased hole logging. Open hole logging involves measuring geological formations in uncased boreholes, providing vital data on reservoir characteristics, fluid content, and rock properties. In contrast, cased hole logging is conducted in wells with casing, focusing on production monitoring and reservoir evaluation. Both methods play crucial roles in oil and gas exploration and production, aiding in resource assessment, well optimization, and informed decision-making for operators.</p></p>
