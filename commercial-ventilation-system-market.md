<p><ul><li>Exhaust Ventilation System</li><li>Supply Ventilation System</li><li>Balanced Ventilation System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial ventilation system market consists of three main types: Exhaust Ventilation Systems remove stale air and pollutants, creating negative pressure to draw in fresh air from outside. Supply Ventilation Systems introduce fresh air into a space, often using filters to enhance air quality. Balanced Ventilation Systems combine both approaches, providing equal air supply and exhaust to maintain indoor air quality and pressure balance. Each type addresses specific requirements for air circulation and quality in commercial environments.</p></p>
