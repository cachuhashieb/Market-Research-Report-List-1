<p><p>The desktop sterilizers market encompasses various types, each utilizing distinct sterilization methods. Steam sterilizers employ high-pressure steam to eradicate microorganisms, making them effective for heat-resistant instruments. Plasma sterilizers use low-temperature hydrogen peroxide plasma, ideal for heat-sensitive items. Hot air sterilizers operate through dry heat, suitable for items that can withstand elevated temperatures. Other sterilization methods may include chemical sterilizers or UV sterilizers. Each type serves specific applications in healthcare, laboratories, and dental sectors, addressing diverse sterilization needs.</p></p>
