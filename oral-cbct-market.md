<p><p>The Oral CBCT market is categorized based on field of view (FOV) sizes: Big FOV, Medium FOV, and Small FOV. Big FOV scanners capture comprehensive 3D images of the entire jaw, making them ideal for complex cases and surgical planning. Medium FOV machines focus on specific regions, suitable for implants and endodontic procedures. Small FOV units provide high-resolution imagery of localized areas, perfect for precise diagnostics and small-scale treatments. Each type addresses distinct clinical needs in dental imaging.</p></p>
