<p><p>The Scanning Contrast Injector market is segmented into three types based on the number of chambers: single-chamber, two-chamber, and three-chamber injectors. Single-chamber injectors accommodate one contrast agent, ideal for basic imaging needs. Two-chamber injectors allow for simultaneous delivery of two different agents, enhancing imaging effectiveness. Three-chamber injectors are versatile, enabling the administration of multiple agents and bolus tracking, which facilitates complex diagnostic procedures. Each type caters to varying clinical needs and imaging methodologies in medical settings.</p></p>
