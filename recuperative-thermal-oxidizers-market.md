This technology is particularly beneficial for industries such as chemical manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, and food processing, where stringent emissions regulations are in place.</p><p>The recuperative thermal oxidizers market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing environmental regulations aimed at reducing air pollution and enhancing energy efficiency.
