<p><strong>The Common Mode Inductor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SMD Common Mode Inductor</li><li>Through Hole Common Mode Inductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The common mode inductor market is primarily segmented into two types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) common mode inductors and through hole common mode inductors. SMD common mode inductors are designed for compact applications and are mounted directly onto the surface of printed circuit boards, offering space efficiency and ease of automation in assembly. In contrast, through hole common mode inductors are inserted into drilled holes on the PCB, providing robust mechanical stability and are often used in higher power applications. Both types are critical in noise filtering and signal integrity enhancement.</p></p>
