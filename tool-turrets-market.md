<p><strong>The Tool Turrets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Disc-Type</li><li>Head-Type</li><li>Crown Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tool turrets are categorized into three main types based on their design and functionality. Disc-type turrets feature a rotating disc to hold multiple tools, allowing for efficient tool changes during machining. Head-type turrets have a stationary head with tools that can be extended or retracted for specific operations, providing versatility in setups. Crown-type turrets incorporate multiple tools in a circular arrangement, optimizing space and enabling quick transitions between tasks. Each type enhances productivity and precision in various manufacturing processes.</p></p>
