<p><ul><li>Gas-insulated</li><li>Vacuum-insulated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The primary switchgear market encompasses two main types: gas-insulated switchgear (GIS) and vacuum-insulated switchgear (VIS). GIS utilizes sulfur hexafluoride gas for insulation, providing compact and reliable solutions ideal for urban substations and areas with limited space. Conversely, VIS employs a vacuum to insulate the circuits, offering enhanced safety and low maintenance due to minimal wear over time. Both technologies cater to diverse applications, ensuring efficient power distribution and management in various industrial and utility environments.</p></p>
