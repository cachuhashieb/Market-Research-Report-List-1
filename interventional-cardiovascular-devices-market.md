<p><p>The interventional cardiovascular devices market includes various categories essential for diagnosing and treating heart conditions. Imaging systems provide real-time visualization of the cardiovascular anatomy, aiding in precise procedures. Catheters are flexible tubes used to navigate through blood vessels, while PTCA balloons are inflated to open narrowed arteries. Stents are mesh-like structures that maintain artery patency. PTCA guidewires facilitate catheter placement and maneuvering. Other devices may include occlusion devices, sheath introducers, and hemostatic valves, diversifying treatment options in cardiovascular care.</p></p>
