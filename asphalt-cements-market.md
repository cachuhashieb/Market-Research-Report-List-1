<p><p>The asphalt cements market encompasses three main types: coal tar pitch, asphalt, and natural asphalt. </p><p>Coal tar pitch is a byproduct of coal carbonization, primarily used in industrial applications and as a binder in road construction. Asphalt, derived from petroleum refining, is the most common form, utilized in paving and roofing due to its waterproofing properties. Natural asphalt, found in natural deposits, is used in similar applications but is less processed. Each type serves specific needs in construction and infrastructure development.</p></p>
