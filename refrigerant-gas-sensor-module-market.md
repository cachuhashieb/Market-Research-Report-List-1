<p><strong>The Refrigerant Gas Sensor Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) Sensors</li><li>Metal Oxide Semiconductor Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refrigerant gas sensor module market features two primary types: NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) sensors and Metal Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors. NDIR sensors detect specific gases by measuring infrared light absorption, providing high accuracy and stable readings, ideal for refrigerant detection. In contrast, MOS sensors rely on changes in electrical conductivity in the presence of gases, offering cost-effectiveness and compact design but potentially lower accuracy. Both types serve critical roles in areas like HVAC systems, leak detection, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
