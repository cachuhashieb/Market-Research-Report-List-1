<p><strong>The Rail Signalling Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traditional Train Control</li><li>Communication Based Train Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rail signalling systems market comprises primarily two types: Traditional Train Control (TTC) and Communication-Based Train Control (CBTC). TTC typically involves mechanical signals and dedicated tracks to manage train movements, relying heavily on fixed infrastructure for safety and efficiency. In contrast, CBTC leverages real-time communication technologies to transmit data between trains and control centers, allowing for more flexible and efficient operations, shorter headways, and improved safety. This shift towards CBTC enhances network capacity and overall service reliability.</p></p>
