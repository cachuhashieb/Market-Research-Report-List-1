<p><p>The adenoid sponges market is divided into two main types: strung and un-strung adenoid sponges. Strung adenoid sponges are pre-attached to a suture for easy handling and placement during surgical procedures, enhancing precision and convenience. In contrast, un-strung adenoid sponges come without any attachment, allowing for flexibility in application but requiring additional tools for secure placement. The choice between the two types often depends on the specific requirements of the surgical procedure and surgeon preference.</p></p>
