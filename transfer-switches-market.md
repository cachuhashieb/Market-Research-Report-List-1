<p><ul><li>Automatic Transfer Switches</li><li>Manual Transfer Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transfer switch market comprises two main types: Automatic Transfer Switches (ATS) and Manual Transfer Switches (MTS). ATS are designed to automatically switch to a backup power source during an outage, ensuring seamless power continuity for critical loads. They are commonly used in commercial and industrial settings. In contrast, MTS require manual intervention to switch to a backup source, offering a more cost-effective solution for smaller applications. Both types improve power reliability but cater to different user needs and operational requirements.</p></p>
