<p><ul><li>Pre-fabricated Softwall Cleanrooms</li><li>Pre-fabricated Hardwall Cleanrooms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pre-fabricated cleanroom market is divided into two main types: softwall and hardwall cleanrooms. Pre-fabricated softwall cleanrooms feature flexible fabric walls, making them easily adjustable and suitable for various applications, particularly in smaller spaces. In contrast, pre-fabricated hardwall cleanrooms consist of rigid panels providing enhanced structural integrity and better contamination control, ideal for industries requiring strict cleanroom standards. Both types offer quick installation, cost-effectiveness, and modular designs, catering to diverse sectors such as pharmaceuticals, electronics, and biotech.</p></p>
