<p><ul><li>Folding Field Cultivator</li><li>Fixed Field Cultivator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The field cultivator market is divided into two primary types: folding and fixed field cultivators. Folding field cultivators are designed with hydraulic mechanisms that allow them to fold for easy transport, making them ideal for larger farms requiring mobility and efficiency. In contrast, fixed field cultivators are stationary and typically have a simpler design, offering robust performance for soil preparation on smaller plots. Both types enhance soil aeration, weed control, and seedbed preparation, catering to different agricultural needs.</p></p>
