<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Fecal Occult Blood (FOBT) Rapid Diagnostic Test Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Immunochemical Fecal Occult Blood Test (iFOBT or FIT)</li><li>Guaiac Fecal Occult Blood Test (gFOBT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fecal Occult Blood Test (FOBT) rapid diagnostic test market is divided primarily into two types: Immunochemical Fecal Occult Blood Test (iFOBT or FIT) and Guaiac Fecal Occult Blood Test (gFOBT). iFOBT utilizes antibodies to detect human hemoglobin in stool samples, offering higher specificity and sensitivity for colorectal cancer screening. In contrast, gFOBT employs a chemical reaction to identify blood, but is less specific, as it can produce false positives from dietary factors. Both types are crucial for early cancer detection.</p></p>
