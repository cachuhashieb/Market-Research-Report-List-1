<p><p>Metallic couplings are essential components used to connect two shafts in various mechanical systems. The chain couplings feature links that provide flexibility and accommodate misalignment, making them suitable for applications with significant fluctuations. In contrast, gear couplings utilize toothed gears to maintain precise alignment while transmitting torque, making them ideal for high-torque, heavy-duty scenarios. Both types offer durability and efficiency, catering to different operational requirements across diverse industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and machinery.</p></p>
