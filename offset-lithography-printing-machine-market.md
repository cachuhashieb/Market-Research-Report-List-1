<p><p>The Offset Lithography Printing Machine market is divided into two main types: Sheet-Fed Offset Printing Press and Web-Fed Offset Printing Press. Sheet-Fed machines use individual sheets of paper, making them ideal for high-quality prints, short runs, and specialized projects. In contrast, Web-Fed machines utilize large rolls of paper, enabling faster production for high-volume jobs like newspapers and magazines. Both types leverage the offset printing technique, ensuring consistent quality and vibrant colors across various printing applications.</p></p>
