<p><p>Flash cure units are essential in the printing industry for rapidly curing inks and coatings. The market can be categorized into two primary types: quartz and infrared. Quartz flash cure units utilize high-intensity quartz lamps to deliver focused heat, promoting quick curing with minimal heat transfer to substrates. In contrast, infrared flash units employ infrared radiation, heating surfaces more uniformly and gently. Both types enhance efficiency and productivity, catering to diverse printing applications while optimizing cure times for various inks.</p></p>
