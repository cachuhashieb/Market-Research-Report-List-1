<p><strong>The Solvent-free Packaging Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-component</li><li>Two-component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solvent-free packaging adhesive market is categorized into two main types: one-component and two-component adhesives. One-component adhesives are pre-formulated, ready to use without the need for mixing, offering convenience and quicker application. In contrast, two-component adhesives require the combination of a resin and a hardener before use, resulting in stronger bonds but necessitating precise mixing. Both types are preferred in eco-friendly packaging solutions, prioritizing minimal VOC emissions while ensuring effective adhesion in various packaging applications.</p></p>
