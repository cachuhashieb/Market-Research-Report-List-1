<p><p>The Polyisocyanurate insulation market is divided into spray type and foam/board type products. Spray-type insulation involves applying a liquid mixture that expands and hardens, creating a seamless thermal barrier suitable for irregular surfaces. This method offers excellent adhesion and insulation properties. Foam/board type insulation consists of pre-manufactured rigid panels that provide high thermal resistance and are easy to install in various applications, including commercial and residential buildings. Both types are valued for energy efficiency and moisture resistance.</p></p>
