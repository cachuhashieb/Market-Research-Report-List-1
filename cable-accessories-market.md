<p><p>The cable and accessories market is categorized into three main types based on voltage levels: low voltage, medium voltage, and high voltage. Low voltage cables, typically up to 1 kV, are used for residential and commercial electrical systems. Medium voltage cables, ranging from 1 kV to 33 kV, are essential for industrial applications and urban distribution. High voltage cables, exceeding 33 kV, are employed in transmission networks for long-distance power distribution, connecting energy sources to substations and ensuring efficient grid functionality.</p></p>
