<p><p>Anti-return gates for airports are classified into two market types: automatic and non-return. Automatic anti-return gates use advanced technology to detect and control passenger flow, automatically preventing re-entry and ensuring security compliance. These systems often integrate with access control technology for seamless operation. Non-return gates, on the other hand, rely on manual intervention or basic mechanisms to restrict unauthorized access and prevent passengers from returning to restricted areas. Both types serve to enhance security and manage airport traffic effectively.</p></p>
