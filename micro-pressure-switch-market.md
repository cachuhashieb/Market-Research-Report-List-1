<p><p>The micro pressure switch market is categorized into two main types: electronic and mechanical. Electronic type switches utilize advanced sensors and circuits to detect pressure changes and provide accurate control, often featuring digital interfaces and higher sensitivity. In contrast, mechanical type switches rely on physical mechanisms, such as diaphragms or levers, to activate at specific pressure levels, offering simplicity and reliability. Each type serves various industries, including HVAC, automotive, and industrial applications, catering to different performance and operational needs.</p></p>
