<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Recombinant Vaccines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Subunit Recombinant Vaccines</li><li>Attenuated Recombinant Vaccines</li><li>Vector Recombinant Vaccines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The recombinant vaccines market encompasses three main types: </p><p>1. **Subunit Recombinant Vaccines** contain harmless pieces of the target pathogen, typically proteins, which stimulate an immune response without causing disease. </p><p>2. **Attenuated Recombinant Vaccines** use live but weakened pathogens, allowing for a strong immune response while minimizing disease risk. </p><p>3. **Vector Recombinant Vaccines** involve genetically modifying a harmless virus to deliver pathogen genes, prompting an immune response. Each type offers unique advantages in effectiveness and safety for disease prevention.</p></p>
