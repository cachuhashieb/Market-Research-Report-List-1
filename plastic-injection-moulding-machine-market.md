PET injection molding machines are specifically designed for processing polyethylene terephthalate, ideal for producing bottles and containers due to their high clarity and strength. On the other hand, PVC injection molding machines are tailored for polyvinyl chloride, suitable for making durable and flexible products like pipes and fittings. Both types are essential in manufacturing, catering to diverse industrial applications in packaging, automotive, and consumer goods sectors.</p></p>
