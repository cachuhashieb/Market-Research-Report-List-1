<p><strong>The Audio Frequency Oscillator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>R-C Oscillator</li><li>L-C Oscillator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The audio frequency oscillator market primarily consists of R-C oscillators and L-C oscillators. R-C oscillators use resistors and capacitors to generate audio frequencies, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, making them popular in low-frequency applications. In contrast, L-C oscillators utilize inductors and capacitors for resonant frequency generation, providing higher stability and precision, suitable for high-frequency applications. Both types serve various industries, including communications, music production, and electronic equipment, enabling essential functionalities in sound synthesis and signal generation.</p></p>
