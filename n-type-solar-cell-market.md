<p><p>The N-type solar cell market features advanced technologies like Heterojunction (HJT), Tunnel Oxide Passivated Contact (TOPCon), and Interdigitated Back Contact (IBC) cells. HJT cells combine crystalline and amorphous silicon layers for higher efficiency and better performance in low light. TOPCon cells utilize a thin tunnel oxide layer to enhance efficiency and reduce recombination losses. IBC cells feature contacts on the rear side, maximizing light absorption and efficiency. These technologies are driving innovation and competitiveness in the solar industry.</p></p>
