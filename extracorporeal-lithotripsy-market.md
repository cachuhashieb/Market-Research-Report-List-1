<p><p>The Extracorporeal Lithotripsy market comprises three primary techniques: Electrohydraulic, Electromagnetic, and Piezoelectric. The Electrohydraulic technique generates shock waves via electrical discharges in water, creating effective stone fragmentation. The Electromagnetic technique uses electromagnetic coils to produce shock waves directed at kidney stones, known for its precision and efficiency. The Piezoelectric technique relies on piezoelectric crystals to generate shock waves, delivering focused energy for stone disintegration. Each method offers unique advantages in treating urinary stones, driving market growth.</p></p>
