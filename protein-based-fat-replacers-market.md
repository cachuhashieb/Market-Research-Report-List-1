<p><p>The protein-based fat replacers market includes types like microparticulated protein and modified whey protein concentrate. Microparticulated proteins are created through high-pressure processing, resulting in small protein particles that mimic fat's mouthfeel and texture, enhancing product creaminess without adding calories. Modified whey protein concentrate is altered to improve solubility and emulsification properties, making it ideal for low-fat and fat-free formulations. Both types are used in a variety of food products, offering nutritional benefits while reducing fatty content.</p></p>
