<p><p>The area lighting system market is categorized into three main types based on pole height: High Pole Street Lights, Medium Post Lights, and Low Post Lights. High Pole Street Lights, typically over 15 feet, are designed for extensive illumination in large open areas like highways and sports fields. Medium Post Lights, ranging from 10 to 15 feet, are ideal for parking lots and parks. Low Post Lights, below 10 feet, are suited for walkways and residential areas, providing focused and ambient lighting.</p></p>
