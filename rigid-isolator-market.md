<p><ul><li>Negative Pressure Isolator</li><li>Positive Pressure Isolator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rigid isolator market encompasses two main types: negative pressure isolators and positive pressure isolators. Negative pressure isolators are designed to contain hazardous materials, preventing their release into the environment, making them ideal for handling infectious agents. Conversely, positive pressure isolators provide a controlled environment with a higher internal pressure, protecting sensitive products from external contamination. Both types are crucial in pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and research settings, ensuring safety and integrity in handling potent compounds and sterile products.</p></p>
