<p><p>The Bakeable Ultra-High Vacuum Valve market features two primary types: gate valves and angle valves. Gate valves are designed to provide a tight seal and efficient flow control, making them ideal for applications requiring minimal leakage in ultra-high vacuum environments. Angle valves, on the other hand, facilitate fluid flow at a 90-degree angle, suitable for space-constrained setups. Both types are essential for maintaining high vacuum integrity in various scientific and industrial applications, ensuring optimal performance and reliability.</p></p>
