<p><ul><li>Phase Angle Control</li><li>Integral Cycle Switching</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Three Phase SCR Power Controller market encompasses two primary types: Phase Angle Control and Integral Cycle Switching. Phase Angle Control modulates power by adjusting the point in the AC cycle at which SCRs conduct, allowing for fine control of power delivery and efficient energy usage. In contrast, Integral Cycle Switching controls power by turning the SCRs on and off for entire cycles, offering simplified control mechanisms and reduced heating. Both methods enhance efficiency, making them vital in industrial applications.</p></p>
