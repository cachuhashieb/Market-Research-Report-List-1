<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Grapple</li><li>Mechanical Grapple</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The scrap grapple market is primarily divided into two types: hydraulic grapples and mechanical grapples. Hydraulic grapples utilize fluid power to open and close their claws, allowing for precise handling of heavy materials and facilitating efficient loading and unloading. In contrast, mechanical grapples rely on mechanical linkages to operate, typically offering a more straightforward design and lower maintenance needs. Both types are essential in various industries, including construction and recycling, for effectively managing and transporting scrap materials.</p></p>
